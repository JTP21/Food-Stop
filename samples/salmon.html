Baked Salmon with
Amazing Lemon Sauce


Delicious baked salmon with crispy potatoes,
broccoli, and the most amazing life-changing
lemon sauce smothered on top of it all. This
is restaurant bistro-style eating!



The combination of savory Salmon and golden brown potatoes

This lemon sauce is a showstopper. It’s creamy and rich and light all at the same time. It starts with shallots and gets infused with a few sprigs of fresh thyme and finished little sizzle of lemon juice, and it feels like there should be more with a to explain but really, that is the beauty of it. It’s simple and it’s got really beautiful, gentle, universally
delicious flavor and just MWAH.

A touch of Tender baby broccoli with a little crunchy char. A mix of Baked salmon simply salted, peppered, brushed with olive oil, and baked to flaky perfection.

The herby, creamy, lemony lemon sauce is delicious. However it really plays more as the supporting role.

Anyone Can cook this!

Anyone Can Cook! - this is one of the famous lines of Gusteau. This meal is very easy to cook. Even those who are not used to cooking on a daily basis can cook this delicious meal. This is great for those who are willing to suprise their special someone on a dinner date at home. So easy to prepare that it even comes great with wine.

How to cook this savory meal

You must first bake potatoes, broccoli, and salmon on a sheet pan. They each must separate cooking times. I find the salmon cooks more evenly and more predictably than on the stovetop where I’m always left wondering if it’s overcooked or just fine Secondly, and ideally while things are roasting, get out a pan and make already the lemon sauce. The mix consists of herbs, the shallots, the creaminess with the zip of lemon.

Place your roasted goodness onto a plate or shallow bowl and liberally cover with sauce. The lemon sauce is intended for the salmon, mostly, but potatoes and broccoli straight up dunked in leftover sauce is next-level.

This is a easy dish to make, and I think you should start considering on cooking this savory meal.
