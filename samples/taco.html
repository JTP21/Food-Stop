Smoky Beans & Greens
Tacos with Aji Verde


Three cheers for summertime eating! Smoky
black beans sautéed with kale and garlic and spices,
tucked into charred corn tortillas, and topped with
the best feisty, punchy green sauce – Aji Verde!



A superb experience of mexican cuisine

These tacos is what makes me shout FLAVOR! Smoky chipotle black beans, sautéed with kale and garlic and spices, tucked into charred corn tortillas, topped with fresh, crisp sweet corn cut right off the cob, and smothered with too much aji verde.

These are just so delightful. They appear humble with the black bean, or kale vibes but seriously, don’t sleep on these. They are a top summertime recipe for me. I had served these to myself, and a random sampling of loved ones and friends. Everyone has given them the approval of awesomeness, and more importantly, everyone has confirmed that the star is the aji verde.

So what is Aji Verde

Aji verde is a spicy Peruvian green sauce, and it came into me when I made it as a little drizzle for a delicious stew. Now here we are, and tacos are in, so I’m revisiting the beloved aji verde for these beans and greens tacos. Because what’s taco without a good sauce? Well actually I don't know because I always want sauce.

Tacos Assemble!

As with all tacos, I WOULD ADVISE YOU, go big with your toppings. You know I will approve of the more-is-more mentality here. For one, I can confirm that avocado is the BOMB. The sweet corn that I scooped all over my tacos was a nice compliment as it brings more sweet, fresh, and a little bit crunchy.

However, you seriously need the Aji Verde. Holy smoke show, these were so good. Three cheers for vegetarian tacos that you can look forward to eating and serving to your friends.
