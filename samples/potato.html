Baked Potatoes through the next stage

These baked potatoes are paired with a creamy mixture of garlic herb spinach and potato, and then loaded with BBQ chicken. It has also caramelized little pan roasted mushrooms, bleu cheese crumbles, crispy onions, and chives. This meal is Crunchy. Saucy. Creamy, tangy, and so sweet. The flavors makes you go into cloud 9. These baked potatoes go big, messy, and so much excessive.

This looks like so intense to prepare but it is actually easy to cook. The components you need for this delicious potato-tastic masterpiece does not need that much tinkering in the kitchen. I can tell that even a meal like this that looks superb can be cooked with not much challenge.

How to cook this meal full of flavors and wonder

Cooking this overloaded meal is so easy. You're just gonna need potatoes, chicken, mushrooms, cheese, spinach sauce, and barbeque sauce. Then you can also use other toppings such as onion chives.

As a starter, you need to bake the potatoes. Next is you're gonna bake the chicken and mushrooms. Then you have to mix the potato along with the spinach filling. After doing it, you already assemble all of it. What is amazing about this meal is that it can be dine in either breakfast, lunch, or dinner. You can even use leftover BBQ Chicken and potato skins for a breakfast meal, which is something flavroful to spice up the day.

This entree is so creamy, packed with flavor, loaded with feel-good veggies, and perfectly spicy-sweet thanks to the BBQ sauce. You need to eat this I tell you.
