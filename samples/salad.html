Cashew Crunch Salad
with Sesame Dressing


This Cashew Crunch Salad is loaded up with crunchy veg,
chow mein noodles, edamame, and roasted cashews and
it’ll make you ACTUALLY WANT TO EAT A SALAD. And
the sesame dressing – OMG!



The salad that checks all the boxes

This salad has all the taste we could want in a salad. Everything I like to eat are present in this collaboration of vegetables, fruits, and dressings.

What is the secret behind this sesame dressing

This gullible dressing consists of many ingredients that sums up its deliciousness. The oil-and-vinegar dressing that you mix up, sweetened up with some sugar, seasoned with sesame oil, that can be delightly added with Greek yogurt or mayo. I don’t know, but this vinaigrette-drenched salad isn’t going to keep me full. I need some more creaminess in the salads, so I just took a delicious dressing and made it more creamier.

Everything you want can be followed

You can make this salad your own way. This is one of the things I like about this salad because you can put any combination you want. For those vegetarians, you can leave out the creaminess. People who like to eat gluten-free kinds of food, can not add the chow mein noodles that has been the factor of its crunchiness. For protein-enthusiasts, you can add any meat with it such as chicken or pork. Maybe you can also add tofu? I don't know but you can make it something you can serve for dinner also.

If you are making this, you must need to chop and prep all the veggies in advance. You also need to even mix them together. Then you can do the mixing of it in the chow mein, cashews, and dressing until just before serving.
