Harissa Chickpeas
with Whipped Feta


Harissa Chickpeas with Whipped Feta! Perfectly
spicy/saucy/tomato-y chickpeas smothered in
creamy, garlicky whipped feta and served with
naan, lemons, and cucumbers. Your next
weeknight wonder!


A little burst of sweet, This was soooooooo Good

Let me serve you up some spicy, saucy, garlicky smashed chickpeas over a piece of soft flatbread with a magic of whipped feta and a crunchy little waterfall of cucumbers over the top.

How to make this delicious meal

All you need for the chickpeas are: harissa paste, garlic, canned crushed tomatoes, olive oil, canned chickpeas. You must first lightly smash the chickpeas. Use a potato masher to give them just a few good smooshes so that the texture will be into something more sticky and saucy. This meal is meal-prep friendly and can be served with flatbread, rice, roasted vegetables, and more. Then you can already add the WHIPPED FETA, which is something that adds up to the deliciousness of this flavorful entree.

Options for more sauce

For emergencies! you can use a plain yogurt as the creamy element. It will save the time and the dishes from the whipped feta. However, if you have so many time to prepare this, you can mix feta into something of a yummy sauce. You have so many options to choose from on what combination do you want. Well, as long as its yummy or if the flavor compliments each one.

The mixture of these obtained good reviews for those I served them to. Chickpeas has the right amount of spicy, that even kids can enjoy it also. However, the kids said that they enjoyed the cucumbers and the whipped feta more. The good thing with this entree is that you can also mix it with leftovers, still making something into a flavorful experience while dining.
